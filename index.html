Common questions

Why am I seeing this referral option?

Your LDL-C result is in the red FH review zone. A GP can check your full result, family history, and medication context before deciding on referral.

Does this mean I have FH?

No. This app flags risk only. Familial hypercholesterolemia needs clinical review and, if appropriate, genetic assessment.

Why does early testing matter?

Earlier review can lead to earlier treatment and may help protect close family members through cascade screening.

Can I self-refer?

No. The GP remains the clinical decision-maker and sends the referral if it is appropriate.

Familial hypercholesterolemia

What this result could mean

FH is an inherited condition that can cause high LDL cholesterol from a young age. Many people feel well, so a high LDL result can be an early signal to discuss with a GP.

  • Bring this result to your GP review.
  • Share any known family history of high cholesterol or early heart disease.
  • Ask whether Genetic Assessment Centre referral is appropriate.
